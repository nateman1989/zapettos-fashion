Suit Tailoring

At Zapettos, we create all of our garments in our local shops. Because we work locally, we are able to invite our clients back for multiple fitting as their garment is being created. The result of this extra care and attention is clothing of unparalleled fit and comfort.

Zapettos uses traditional English practice in the creation of bespoke garments. This means that jackets will have a more trim cut, with a tapered waist and less padding in the shoulders. Jackets are fully canvased, including in the collar and chest, with handwork used to pad the collar with felt, roll the lapels, and sew the buttonholes. From start to finish our Chief Tailors and their dedicated fashion teams will walk you through the many steps that go into the making of a true bespoke garment.
